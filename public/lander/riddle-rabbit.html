Riddle Rabbit

Delve into the succes story of how our team has gone through the challenges & developed this app successfully

The Challenge There were 2 prime challenges that we had which are not a very big thing now, but needed serious attention at that point of time when the technological resources were less

Voice and click synchronization (while question reading and right/wrong click)

Single device screen which needed to look perfect on all screen sizes

Solutions From AppsThink As an achievable mindset, we came forward and accepted the challenge. We worked with more than 100 riddles and successfully covered all the milestones and challenges.

The first thing was to achieve extraordinary and breathtaking User Interface with single screen design which must work on all types of devices and should also hold a very less device space. Once this was done, then our next achievement was to synchronize all the voices with user's response in each riddle.

Not just that, we never stopped in achieving one thing. We proceeded with multi-level testing which included eclipse, unit testing, QTP, and many more

Outcome For us, it is always new achievement in learning.

For client, they were able to cover up a new set of users, prep school children.

Also, we successfully achieved Creative Child Magazine award in 2014 in the Problem Solving Thinking Apps Category.
