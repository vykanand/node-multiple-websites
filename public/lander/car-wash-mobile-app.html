Car grooming at your doorsteps

Car cleaning app offers professional car cleaning services at your office or home. Just call up immediately whenever you want, make quick bookings, & see your car shine like a new one. Enjoy car wash experience with advanced solutions delivered by well-equipped car groomers.

Contact Sales
  •  

    User sign up & log-in

    Switch to the user-friendly Car cleaning app to enjoy standard car wash services at your location. Create your account for user registration & log-in to access an online portal to maintain your car’s cleanliness.

  •  

    Location setup

    Get on-demand car wash services at your doorsteps. Set your location or simply mention your address and book your appointment for car wash up. A well-equipped washer will pick your car for the dry-clean processes & deliver it to any location as desired by you.

  •  

    Appointment details

    Once the booking for a car wash is confirmed, a user can check out appointment details on the screens. One can view the options for scheduled & immediate bookings along with the booked location.

  •  

    Track order completion

    Car cleaning app facilitates convenience to stay updated with your order progress report. You can check out the real-time progress of your order along with the entire schedule & timings.
